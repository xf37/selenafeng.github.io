Selena (Xin) Feng

About Me

    I am a Ph.D. in Geography (Spatial Data Science) from the University of California - Santa Barbara and plan to graduate in 2019 Fall. My research revolves spatial optimization and statistical modeling (including location & allocation models, routing optimization, regression models, and clustering), with applications to medical drone delivery, meetup location finding, and nighttime light coverage. I have deep expertise with analyzing both structured and unstructured large-scale geospatial data. I have published 10+ peer-reviewed papers with 150+ total citations. Outside academia, I worked as a Data Scientist Intern at Oak Ridge National Laboratory and was recently a Data Science Fellow at Insight Data Science (Silicon Valley) . Furthermore, I have extensive hands-on experience in machine learning and its applications, winning top 1% in two Kaggle competitions collaboratively. I am fluent in Python, Matlab, Gurobi, R, and SQL.
